### **spring cloud nacos**

[微服务注册配置中心使用说明_微服务引擎(MSE)-阿里云帮助中心](https://help.aliyun.com/zh/mse/getting-started/usage-notes?spm=a2c4g.11186623.0.i1)

[快速开始-阿里云Spring Cloud Alibaba官网](https://sca.aliyun.com/docs/2023/user-guide/nacos/quick-start/)

[Nacos 融合 Spring Cloud，成为注册配置中心 | Nacos 官网](https://nacos.io/docs/v2.5/ecology/use-nacos-with-spring-cloud/?spm=5238cd80.2ef5001f.0.0.3f613b7cobygKZ)

作为配置中心和注册中心

```
spring:
  cloud:
    nacos:
      config:
        server-addr: ${server_discovery_config_addr}:8848
        namespace: config
        group: ${server_discovery_group:DEFAULT_GROUP}
        file-extension: yml
      discovery:
        server-addr: ${server_discovery_config_addr}:8848
        namespace: ${server_discovery_namespace}
        register-enabled: ${discovery_register_enable}
```

注意自动更新配置需要`@Refreshscope`注解
